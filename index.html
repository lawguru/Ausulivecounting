About the Portal

The AUSU Vote Counting Portal is designed to provide accurate, transparent, and up-to-date results of all student union and committee elections within AUSU. This platform ensures that students, faculty, and stakeholders can easily monitor the progress of vote counting in multiple categories with full confidence.

Vote Counting Sections

Student Council Elections

Count and monitor votes for the AUSU Student Council posts including President, Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer.

Sports Committee

Track election results for the Sports Committee representatives who will promote sports activities across campus.

Cultural Council

Stay updated with the vote count for cultural committee posts organizing events and festivals.

Technical Society

Follow the live counting for the Technical Society posts responsible for tech events and workshops.

Why Real-Time Vote Counting Matters

Transparent vote counting builds trust in the election process and empowers students to be actively involved. Our portal guarantees timely updates so you never miss a single vote count. It also reduces disputes by providing clear, official data accessible to everyone.
